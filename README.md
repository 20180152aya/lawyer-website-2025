# Basma Gamal Law Office - Official Website

This is a professional website for the law office of **Basma Gamal**. The website showcases the office's legal services, provides legal blog articles, and includes a contact page that allows clients to send messages directly to the office email via EmailJS.

## Description

The website is designed to provide a full overview of the **Basma Gamal Law Office** in a professional and interactive way. It includes:

- **Homepage:** Highlights main legal services and expertise.
- **Why Choose Us Section:** Explains the advantages and experience of the law office.
- **Services Section:** Displays all legal services offered by the office.
- **Legal Blog:** Offers informative legal articles using an interactive accordion.
- **Contact Page:** Clients can send messages directly to the office email. Includes a Google Map showing the office location.

---

## Features

- Fully responsive and professional design using **React.js** and **Bootstrap**.
- Smooth scroll animations and effects using **AOS (Animate On Scroll)**.
- Direct email sending to the office using **EmailJS** (no backend required).
- Detailed listing of all legal services offered.
- Legal blog with collapsible articles for interactive reading.
- Contact form with input validation and success/error notifications.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** React.js  
- **CSS & UI:** Bootstrap, Custom CSS  
- **Animations:** AOS (Animate On Scroll)  
- **Email Sending:** EmailJS (Browser SDK)  
- **Routing:** react-router-dom
